Global SuperStore

Sales Performance Dashboard

2011-2014
Business Problem
The business is growing, but profit is leaking across markets, categories, and discount policies.

The goal is to identify where sales generate healthy profit and where commercial decisions create losses.

Analysis Task
Analyze sales, profit, margin, discounts, and loss concentration.

The dashboard summarizes performance by year, market, category, discount bucket, country, and product.

Methods
Data cleaning, aggregation, KPI reporting, and interactive filtering.

The source data was cleaned in Python, aggregated into analysis tables, and published as a static dashboard.

Key Result
High discounts are the main profitability risk.

Discounts above 20% often turn margins negative. The recommendation is to tighten discount policy and review weak markets and products.
